Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 June 2025

At TechFix Nigeria, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains in plain language how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our website and services in Nigeria. We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) and other relevant Nigerian laws.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information You Provide:
    • Your name, email address, and contact details when submitting a complaint or registering.
    • Device information (e.g., type, brand, model) and description of your tech issue.
    • Any additional information you send us via forms, email, or chat.
  • Login Information:
    • If you log in with Google, we collect basic profile details (name, email address, and profile photo).
  • Automatically Collected Data:
    • Device type, browser type, pages visited, IP address, and usage statistics-collected via cookies and analytics tools to help us improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and respond to your complaints and service requests.
  • To match your issue to the right expert technician.
  • To communicate updates about your service or complaint status.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To comply with Nigerian law, legal requests, or protect our rights.
  • For safety, fraud prevention, and to ensure fair use of our platform.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • With Trusted Troubleshooters: Only the information needed to resolve your issue is shared with certified technicians.
  • With Service Providers: We may use third-party companies (like web hosts, analytics, or email providers) who process data on our behalf. All are required to protect your information.
  • Legal & Safety: We may share your data if required by Nigerian law or to protect you, us, or others against fraud or harm.
  • We do not sell your personal information to anyone.

4. How We Protect Your Information

  • Your data is stored securely using modern encryption and access controls.
  • Only authorised TechFix Nigeria staff and troubleshooters have access to your personal information for legitimate reasons.
  • We regularly review our security practices and train our staff on data privacy.

5. Your Rights & Choices (Nigeria)

  • You can request access to your information, ask us to correct inaccurate data, or delete your data at any time.
  • You may withdraw consent for certain uses of your data (such as marketing) at any time.
  • To exercise your rights, email us at support@techfix.ng. We will respond within 7 working days, as required by Nigerian law.

6. Cookies & Analytics

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to keep our website secure, remember your preferences, and analyze site usage.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some site features may not work correctly if you do.

7. Data Storage & International Transfers

  • Your data is stored on secure servers in Nigeria or countries with equivalent data protection standards.
  • If we need to transfer data outside Nigeria, we ensure it is protected under Nigerian law and, where relevant, international agreements.

8. Children’s Privacy

  • Our services are not intended for children under 13 years old.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we’ll notify you by updating this page and, if necessary, via email or on our homepage.

Please check this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
